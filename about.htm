About Us

We specialise in all kinds of classic car restoration. With our team of seven expert craftsmen, we can undertake just about every form of classic car restoration project.

From sprucing up the paintwork of a newer classic in our two Dalby paint booths to a complete re-build of a one-of-a-kind vehicle hand built in another era. Using heritage techniques and methods that would have made your vehicle’s original engineers proud, we can methodically restore every nut and bolt of your classic car using tried and tested methods or the latest technology.

We believe every classic car restoration deserves to be treated like a masterpiece, and in our fully equipped workshop, our craftsmen have the skills and experience to give your classic car the chance to live out its full potential.
